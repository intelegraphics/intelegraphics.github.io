App Support And User Privacy

This app respects and protects your privacy. This policy aims to clearly explain how we handle information related to you.
1. Core Commitment: We Do Not Collect Your Personal Information
No Collection of Personal Information: We solemnly declare that this product does not actively collect, store, use, or transmit any of your personal information in any form. This includes, but is not limited to:
- Personally identifiable information such as your name, address, phone number, or email.
- Account security details including usernames, passwords, or payment information.
- Location data, device identifiers (e.g., IMEI, IDFA), or IP addresses.
- Usage data like browsing history, search queries, input content, or uploaded files.
- Any other data that could directly or indirectly identify you.

No Third-Party SDKs:
This product does not integrate any third-party software development kits (SDKs), eliminating any possibility of indirect data collection through such means.

2. Technical Information We Process

We do not collect any personal information from users, including but not limited to biometric data or physical device information. We collect no data from you whatsoever.

3. Information Sharing & Disclosure

No Sharing of Personal Information:
Since we collect no personal data, we do not share, sell, rent, or transfer it to any third parties under any circumstances.

4. Information Security

No Personal Data Risks:
As we retain no personal information, there is zero risk of its leakage, loss, or damage due to our operations.

Technical Data Protection:
We implement reasonable measures (HTTPS encryption, server security configurations, regular log audits) to safeguard server and anonymized technical data against unauthorized access or breaches.

5. Your Rights

No Personal Data Rights Apply:
Given our strict no-collection policy, you need not submit requests to access, correct, delete, or port personal data, nor withdraw consent or close accounts.
