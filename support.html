Last Updated: September 1, 2025

Your privacy is important to us. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (referred to as the "App").

Please read this policy carefully before using our services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

Personal Identifiable Information

When you register for an account or purchase a subscription, we may collect:

  • Email address
  • Username

Payment Information

All purchases are processed by the Apple App Store. We do not collect or store any payment information such as your credit card number or bank account details. We only receive an encrypted transaction token from Apple to manage your subscription status. Please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy for details on how they handle your data.

Subscription Data

We collect information related to your subscription, such as:

  • Subscription status (e.g., active, expiration date)

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Providing and Maintaining Services: Creating your account, delivering App features, and managing your subscription.
  • Processing Payments: Handling your subscription purchases and renewals through the Apple App Store.
  • Communicating with You: Sending service-related notices (e.g., subscription confirmation, renewal reminders, major feature updates), customer support responses, and marketing communications (you may opt out at any time).
  • Compliance and Legal Obligations: Complying with applicable laws, regulations, or legal processes.

3. Auto-Renewable Subscriptions

  • Payment Processing: All subscription payments are handled by the Apple App Store.
  • Renewal: Subscriptions will automatically renew unless auto-renewal is turned off at least 24 hours before the end of the current subscription period.
  • Managing Subscriptions: You can manage or cancel your subscription at any time by accessing your Apple Account Settings > "[Your Name]" > "Subscriptions." We cannot directly manage or cancel subscriptions as this process is controlled by Apple.
